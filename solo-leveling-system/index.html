Attribute Glossary

  • STR boosts raw Attack Power. Crucial for slicing down high-HP gate bosses quickly.
  • AGI raises Evasion chance and attack speed, allowing you to avoid damage.
  • VIT increases maximum HP and provides flat defense reduction.
  • SEN spikes Critical Hit Rate, enabling explosive double-damage strikes.
  • INT raises Max MP, unlocking magical damage spikes and Shadow Army slots.
